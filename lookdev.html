look development

Victorian Groove

Victorian Groove is a project that I did in Summer of 2020 where I focused on designing an original boot design with intricate textures and patterns. Check out the video demo!

Programs: Autodesk Maya, Substance Painter

Flap Jackie

Flap Jackie is an animation project that I did as part of my animation class where I did an end-to-end creation of Flap Jackie and her scene. Check out the video demo!

Programs: Autodesk Maya, Substance Painter, Zbrush

Fallen Ship

Fallen Ship is an animation project for ANIM 222_02 where I concentrated on portraying the textures of a treasure chest and the effects of being underwater.

Programs: Autodesk Maya, Substance Painter, Zbrush

Hometree

Hometree is a project I did in Summer 2020. This project was based on a doodle and when I converted it to 3D, I played with the shape language to create a dreamy landscape.

Programs: Autodesk Maya, Substance Painter

Dolly the Dog

Dolly the Dog is a project I did in Summer 2020. Here I am playing with a more flat cartoony styled character based on a doodle I did!

Programs: Autodesk Maya, Substance Painter
